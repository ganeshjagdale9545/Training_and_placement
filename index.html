The Placement Cell plays a crucial role in locating job
opportunities for Under Graduates and Post Graduates
passing out from the college by keeping in touch with
reputed firms and industrial establishments. The Placement
Cell operates round the year to facilitate contacts between
companies and graduates. The number of students placed
through the campus interviews is continuously rising.
On invitation, many reputed industries visit the institute to
conduct interviews.
